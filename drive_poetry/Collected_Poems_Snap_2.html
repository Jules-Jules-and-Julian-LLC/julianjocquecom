Attention Is All You Have Top

Human brains are finite. Human bodies are finite. The Earth is finite. The universe might be finite, spacetime is seeming fixed based on an initial configuration, which means there’s no free will and the only way we can break free is quantum mechanics which seem to be telling us “there’s an information floor to the world” so we keep shaving off the random bits and that’s called observation or consciousness or analog signal processing or physical reality or whatever but it’s really seeming like there’s some fundamental floor at which spacetime binds us from control.

 

We really need to fix this whole death thing, and fast. Why don’t we cryo-freeze ourselves when we’re in peak condition then wait it out? Is that not what eggs and sperm are but unfrozen?

Oddities of my personality Top

Tendency to become obsessed over things. From Ghost to the piano video to This Is America, I get fixated on things for very short periods of time then move on. This has a lot to do with the next point about fantasies of grandeur. I imagine how cool it would be to be knowledgable about those things or skilled in them then get fixated on them.

Related to this, I also tend to focus on things I create. When I put out a Youtube video I’ll watch it over and over again, and I like reading over my old Reddit comments, especially ordered by votes, to see when I said funny or clever things. Similarly, I like having the Quotes channel for D&D to remember good moments I created there.

 

Overly sentimental. I think related to the last point about focusing on my works, I also have a lot of trouble giving things up. This was particularly pronounced when I was a kid but I’ve gotten better about it. However things like photos and videos I hang onto obsessively. I feel like if I lose them I’ll be losing part of my memory.

Very nostalgic. Replay Maplestory almost every year. Likes looking over old photos, videos, and Reddit comments as mentioned above. Partly to fulfill vanity but also reliving a time I can’t go back to. I definitely romanticize a time when life was simpler.

 

Fantasies of grandeur. I often imagine what it would be like to be the best at something or to live an amazing life I’d never have. Thoughts like “what would it be like to be a billionaire?” tend to lead to thoughts like “how can I become a billionaire?” which leads to nagging unrealistic thoughts like “I need to start a business to become truly wealthy. Nobody got truly wealthy working for someone else.”

 

Inattentiveness. My thoughts jump around a lot and I can forget what I was doing very easily. For example, walking to Reamer to get lunch and coming back with only my mail.

 

Previous difficulty with eating regularly. Good about this now but I used to not care about eating or be bothered by being hungry. Resulted in being pretty severely underweight.

 

Toxic as a leader. The few times I’ve had power I put down those that were working under me and wanted to make it clear I was the smartest one there and everyone had to follow my lead. The large scale project where Cara was in the room for our meeting.

 

“Manic” episodes. Stemming out of my tendency to get obsessed. I will have periods of time (sometimes half a day) where I get so focused on something I lose all concept of the outside world. Time slips away and I just do that one thing passionately as if it were the only thing in the world. Like if I get in a groove coding, that’s the only thing I want to do then.

 

Scorched earth approach to toxic relationships. Kit, Taylor Gest, Will Harney. Once I identified them as toxic I cut them out completely and the only time I felt things about it were when I had to deal with them after I made that decision. I wanted them to simply disappear.

 

Seeks admiration. D&D I’m constantly fishing for compliments. My favorite part of my wedding was all the love getting dumped on me.

 

Acute social awareness. I’m very, very deeply aware of what is socially playing out in any situation I’m in. I’m good at identifying what people want and giving it to them so they like me. For example, in general with women I will go out of my way to give them validation since they generally seek it. I think Emily Stout likes me because of that. I give her attention and make her feel smart which she likes. I didn’t like Maddy(?) for that reason, she didn’t care what I thought about her and didn’t seek my validation so I felt snubbed like I couldn’t figure out a way to get her to like me.

 

Extreme shows of emotional support. The thank you cards, but also the letters to my parents on their decade birthdays. I really deeply express how I feel about them and how thankful I am for them.
