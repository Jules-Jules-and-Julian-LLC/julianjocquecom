Frater in Lege (The Great Spotted Birder)
Habitat High Altitude / Rocky Mountains
Plumage Performance Fleece & Flannel
Call "Shh! Did you hear that?"

An independent and swift-moving creature, The Killian covers remarkable distances across rugged terrain, rarely breaking stride. Unlike lesser birders who stake out a single perch, this specimen trusts the wilderness to reveal its wonders along the way—and it reliably does.
