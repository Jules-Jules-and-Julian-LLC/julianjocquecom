How to Transfer Spotify Playlists

Move your playlists from Spotify to other music services using TuneMyMusic.

1

Log In to TuneMyMusic

Go to tunemymusic.com and click Login in the top-right corner.

Username: jocquechristmas@gmail.com
Password: swan1swan!
2

Select Spotify as the Source

Click Spotify as your source.

3

Connect Your Spotify Account

Click Load from Spotify account.

Then click Switch Account, then Add new account.

Why switch accounts? This lets you log in with your own Spotify account so it can read your personal playlists.
4

Authorize Spotify

Log in to your Spotify account and agree to the data sharing prompt. This just lets TuneMyMusic read your playlists.

5

Choose Your Destination

Click Choose Destination and pick where you want your playlists to go:

  • YouTube — Creates playlists on your YouTube account, accessible from the YouTube app
  • YouTube Music — Creates playlists in your YouTube Music account, accessible from the YouTube Music app
Julian's recommendation: I'm going with both YouTube and YouTube Music, because I can access them ad-free on my Android phone for free using ReVanced (see below).
6

Start the Transfer

Click Start Transfer and wait for it to finish. That's it!

How long does it take? Out of about 15,000 tracks, the transfer took around 2 hours and only missed about 250 tracks. Not bad!
