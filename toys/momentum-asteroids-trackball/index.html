Momentum Cutter

A trackball-native toy: spin the ball to build momentum. Smash asteroids to split them smaller and smaller until they pop.

Trackball motion
Adds thrust in the direction you roll. If your trackball keeps spinning, thrust keeps coming.
Stop the ball sharply
Triggers a strong “mag-brake” (no buttons needed). This is the “stop on a dime” move.

Important: For the “infinite fling” feel, this uses the Pointer Lock API (“raw trackball mode”). Click the button below (or the canvas) to lock. Press Esc to unlock.

If it feels too sensitive or too sluggish, adjust Sensitivity once you’re in. On a normal mouse this will feel… kind of awful (by design). On a trackball, it should feel like you’re throwing a puck forever.
